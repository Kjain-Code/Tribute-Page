Mary Kom

Introduction

Mary Kom, a remarkable Indian boxer, stands as a symbol of grit and determination. Her inspiring journey from a humble background to becoming a world champion transcends boundaries. With an indomitable spirit, she has broken barriers and paved the way for women in sports, leaving an indelible mark.

Achievements

MC Mary Kom is the first Indian woman to win a boxing gold at the Asian Games.

Her unwavering dedication and skill have earned her numerous accolades, cementing her as one of the most celebrated and influential athletes in India's history.

  • World Champion

    Six-time World Amateur Boxing Champion

  • Olympic Medalist

    Won a bronze medal at the Olympics

  • Padma Bhushan

    Recipient of the prestigious Padma Bhushan award

Quote

"Don't ever give up. Believe in yourself or nobody will believe in you. My self-motivation and self-belief is my biggest strength." - Mary Kom
